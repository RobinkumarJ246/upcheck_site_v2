## Overview

**Makeathon 7.0** is the flagship innovation-driven hackathon of the **Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)** at **Sri Venkateswara College of Engineering (SVCE)**, Sriperumbudur, Tamil Nadu.

## How It Works

1. Teams register and submit a **PPT idea presentation** by 3rd April 2026.
2. Shortlisted finalists are announced on **7th April 2026**.
3. Confirmed teams complete **payment registration** by 10th April 2026.
4. RSVPs with event details and logistics are sent to all finalists.
5. The hackathon officially **kicks off on 15th April 2026** with an inauguration ceremony.
6. Teams spend **24 hours** building hardware/software solutions around the problem statements.
7. Winners are declared on **16th April 2026** at the valedictory ceremony.

---

## Problem Statements

Problem statements are **real-world, industry-oriented** challenges released on 25th March 2026. Teams pick a problem and build functional prototypes during the 24-hour sprint. Full problem statements are available on the [official website](https://make-a-thon-7.in/#problems).
